Dr. Ifeoma Nwogu
I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and Co-Director of Graduate Studies at UB. I completed my PhD in 2009 at UB, and was an NSF-sponsored postdoctoral researcher at the Computer Science department at the University of Rochester, working with Dr. Chris Brown in Computer Vision. Before that, I completed my master's degree in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania and my Electrical Engineering undergraduate degree at the University of Lagos, Nigeria. My current research interests are in computational social psychology, primarily in developing dynamic statistical modeling to study different forms of human behaviors such as deception detection.
